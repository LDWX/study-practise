Cats deserve a second chance too.

We believe that everyone deserves a second chance. This philosophy has driven us to build the Bay Area's most state of the art facilities where we help cats become the healthiest they can be before they find a new family.

Our Mission

Ferguson's Cat Shelter is a no-kill shelter that takes in cats, helps them become the healthiest cats they can be, and assists in finding new homes. Founded in 2001, Ferguson's Cat Shelter is San Francisco's premiere cat sheleter.
